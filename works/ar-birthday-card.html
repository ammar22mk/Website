AR Birthday Card

Developed in Unity + Vuforia as a mobile AR app with image tracking that transforms photos into video playback.

Project Overview

This project uses Unity and Vuforia image tracking to recognize photo targets and play video directly in AR. The goal was to turn a normal birthday greeting into an interactive mobile experience. I have print out the card in A5 but i already gave the card to the person so the demo video shows the AR experience using the card picture on my PC.

Features

  • Mobile AR app with Vuforia image tracking
  • Transforms tracked photos into video playback in AR
  • 3D rotating birthday cake animation
  • Particle system fireworks effects
  • C# scripted typewriter text effects

Tech Stack

Unity Vuforia AR 3D Interaction

What I Learned

This project gave me hands-on experience with mobile AR development using Unity and Vuforia. I learned how to optimize video playback and particle effects for mobile devices, and how to design intuitive interactions that bridge the physical and digital worlds.
